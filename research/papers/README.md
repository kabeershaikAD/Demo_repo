### Research Focus

PEARL introduces the first attempt to distill orchestration capabilities from large LLMs into small, locally deployable models (Flan-T5-small, 80M parameters) for multi-agent RAG systems.

### Alignment

This research document describes the theoretical framework and research objectives that are implemented in:
- **Project**: `projects/slm_orchestration_legal_rag/`

### Key Contributions

1. **First Distilled Orchestrator**: Flan-T5-small (80M) for multi-agent orchestration
2. **Public Dataset**: First orchestration traces dataset (1,000+ queries)
3. **Evaluation Metrics**: RAS, WAI, RAGAS metrics for orchestration quality
4. **Cost Reduction**: 500x lower cost than GPT-4 orchestration
5. **Latency Improvement**: 15-25× faster than cloud-based orchestration
